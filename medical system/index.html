Emergency Information

In case of emergencies, immediate medical attention is required. Contact the nearest hospital or call your local emergency hotline right away.

General Medicine

General medicine covers routine health checkups, preventive care, and management of common illnesses and chronic conditions.

Cardiology

Cardiology specializes in diagnosing and treating heart conditions such as arrhythmias, coronary artery disease, and heart failure.

Pediatrics

Pediatrics focuses on the health and well-being of children, from newborns to adolescents, ensuring proper growth and development.

Our diagnostic laboratories are outfitted with state-of-the-art equipment to ensure precision and comfort in every examination. Patients of our hospital and clinic have access to:

  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging Laboratory – delivering high-resolution imaging for accurate diagnosis.
  • Computed Tomography Laboratory – offering swift, detailed scans with minimal discomfort.
  • Endoscopy Laboratory – providing advanced internal imaging for precise medical insights.
  • Comprehensive Diagnostic Laboratory – equipped for a wide range of clinical analyses.
  • Ultrasound Laboratory – employing modern ultrasonic techniques for safe, efficient examination.
  • X-ray Laboratory – offering precise imaging with reduced exposure.
  • Stress Testing and HOLTER Monitoring Laboratory – dedicated to thorough cardiovascular assessments.
